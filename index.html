Executive Corporate Structure

We utilize a customized 15-minute level structure with no antes. This optimizes the pace of play for a corporate environment, mathematically bringing the 4-hour tournament to a thrilling conclusion exactly on time.

Inclusive Alternate Seating System:

We are anticipating 350 total attendees with roughly 300 playing. For any guests beyond the initial 300 who wish to play, we provide a seamless alternate system. Each additional player receives a stickered number. As active players are eliminated during the first hour, our dealers (equipped with spare chip racks) notify the floor staff, who will personally call the alternates' numbers and escort them to an open seat. Absolutely no one will feel left out of the action.

AV & Digital Blind Timer:

To keep the action moving, we will provide a Custom Event Digital Blind Timer displayed across 6 TVs. This includes 5 TVs and stands stationed inside the barn (front, back, and sides) and 1 TV stationed outside on the patio under an umbrella to prevent overheating.
